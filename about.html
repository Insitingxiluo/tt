About Us

This website is a demonstration of organizing and displaying a large dataset of tweets by topics using Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA). Each topic is represented by a set of keywords, and the tweets are grouped based on their dominant topics. The site features navigation, comments, and social sharing functionality.
